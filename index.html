The Restaurant: Step into PRIMROSE, a haven of culinary delights and serene ambiance in the heart of Udawalawa, Sri Lanka. Experience the true essence of our culture with an array of mouthwatering dishes, including traditional Sri Lankan Rice and Curry, flavorful Fried Rice, and delectable Noodles.


Our carefully curated menu caters to every palate, promising an unforgettable gastronomic journey. Indulge in a calming atmosphere where you can savor your meal amidst warm hospitality. PRIMROSE - A Taste of Serenity for the whole family to relish.
